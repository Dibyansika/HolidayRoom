Our project in brief

Robot Santa trapped inside your room, programmed to destroy your boredom. It is our website; it contains information about our hardware project, including components used and features of our hardware and our team.

Get to know the features of our device

Warmly wishes you!

The bot connects to a Bluetooth speaker cum mic. It listens through the mic constantly. Converts speech to text using a python script and searches keywords in it to execute commands. If you wish it Merry Christmas, it will reply you back and, it will show merry Christmas on the LCD screen.

Plays Christmas Songs

If you ask it to play a song, it will choose one of the songs randomly from the Christmas files and play one of them. You can even pause songs, stop songs, and resume the songs.

We even have a surprise!

If you ask it to show a Christmas tree, it will show you a Christmas tree on the LCD screen.
